Cricket Betting Types for Limited Overs Matches

  • Match Outcome
    • Match Winner: Bet on which team will win the match.
    • Tie: In rare cases, you might bet on the match ending in a tie.
  • Top Batsman/Bowler
    • Top Run Scorer: Bet on which player will score the most runs for their team.
    • Top Wicket Taker: Bet on which player will take the most wickets in the match.
  • Run Totals
    • Total Runs Scored: Predict the total number of runs scored by a team or by both teams combined.
    • Over/Under Runs: Bet on whether the total runs scored will be over or under a specified number.
  • Individual Performances
    • Player to Score a Half-Century or Century: Bet on whether a specific player will score 50 or 100 runs.
    • Player to Take a Certain Number of Wickets: Bet on whether a specific player will take a certain number of wickets.
  • Method of Dismissal
    • First Wicket Method: Bet on how the first wicket will fall (e.g., bowled, caught, LBW).
    • Top Batsman's Dismissal: Predict how the top run scorer will be dismissed.
  • Innings Runs
    • Innings Runs Total: Predict the total number of runs scored by a team in a particular innings.
    • Highest Scoring Over: Bet on which over will see the most runs scored.
  • Session or Period Betting
    • Top Team at a Specific Interval: Bet on which team will be ahead at a specific point in the match (e.g., after 10 overs).
  • Man of the Match
    • Man of the Match: Bet on which player will be named "Man of the Match" based on their overall performance.
  • Specific Events
    • First Boundary: Bet on which player will hit the first boundary.
    • Highest Partnership: Predict the highest partnership in terms of runs.
  • Team Performance
    • Team to Hit Most Sixes/Fours: Bet on which team will hit more sixes or fours.
    • Team to Score More Runs in the Powerplay: Bet on which team will score more runs in the first 6 overs.
  • Toss Outcome
    • Toss Winner: Bet on which team will win the toss.
